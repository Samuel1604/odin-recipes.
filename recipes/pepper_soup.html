Pepper Soup

Description

Pepper soup is one of the well known Nigerian dishes, it is actually the only soup that is consumed without pairing with other foods. You can, however, paired with boiled yam or rice.

Ingredent

  • Assorted meat
  • Sliced Utazi Leaves
  • Peppersoup spice
  • Stock fish
  • Roasted fish
  • Crayfish
  • Scent leaves
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Clean the ginger and garlic, pound or blend together. The seed of ehu is one of the most relevant ingredients just go ahead and break away the outer shell, then go ahead and blend/pound together with the uziza seeds and half cup of crayfish

  2. Slice the onions and utazi leaves and set aside in a plate. This same ingredient (ehu) is used for making several other Nigerian foods, like the African salad and Nkwobi. If you are preparing pepper soup with assorted meat you want to wash the meat properly with hot water.

  3. If you bought the already-washed bitter leaves from the market already, it is advisable to boil alone for about ten minutes, this would further remove the bitter taste and serve other purposes.


  4. Start cooking the meat with some simple ingredients. (A seasoning cube, a teaspoon of beef seasoning spice, a teaspoon of salt and half cup of sliced onions).


  5. Cook the meat for just 10 minutes then add about 3 cups of water and cook for 30-50 minutes until the meat becomes tender and easy to chew. You can add another cup of water.


  6. Add the pepper soup spice, the ground ehu, uziza, crayfish, and remaining onions. Add the Paprika a teaspoon. Paprika is a spice made from the dried fruit of red bell pepper. It makes my pepper soup somewhat reddish.


  7. Add the sliced utazi, salt and (ordinary) pepper to taste. Allow to boil for another 5-10 minutes and you just made a delicious pot of pepper soup, One of Nigeria’s most popular evening meals.


  8. You can serve alone with a chilled bottle of your favorite drink. Although, I enjoy it better with boiled white rice or yam. So this is how to prepare pepper soup with intestine, tripe, liver and plain red beef – Assorted meat.
